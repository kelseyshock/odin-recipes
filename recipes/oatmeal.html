Yummy Oatmeal

In the works of Eco, a predominant concept is the concept of postcapitalist consciousness. In a sense, the subject is contextualised into a subtextual theory that includes culture as a totality. Many deappropriations concerning materialist neodialectic theory exist.

Ingredients:

  • 1c oatmeal, rolled or large flake
  • -- oat milk, more for large flake, less for rolled
  • -- fruit, vanilla, brown sugar, syrup, chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Put optional frozen fruit in a large pan and cook until thawed
  2. Add milk and oats and brown sugar/syrup/vanilla
  3. Cook until oats are soft and serve hot with toppings
